Help
River Path: Click to add points. Close shape by clicking first point or press Enter. Draws river area — land is auto-generated outside.

Poly Obstacle: Click to add points for a custom-shaped obstacle. Close by clicking first point or Enter. Any shape!

Select: Click item to select. Drag to move. Corner handles resize. Circle handle above = rotate.

Scroll wheel on selected asset = rotate by 15°
Finish/Start: Click two points to draw the line. Auto-detects horizontal vs vertical based on your two clicks. Drag to reposition. Edit axis in properties.

Slow Zone: Draw polygon area that halves boat speed. Invisible in game — place assets on top for visuals.

Spawn Point: Click to place exact boat spawn position. Drag to reposition. Set angle in properties.
